# Data Deletion Request for Varisankya

**Last updated: January 1, 2026**

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from the Varisankya app, please follow these steps:

1.  Send an email to **aarshps@gmail.com** with the subject line: **"Account Deletion Request"**.
2.  Please send the email from the same address linked to your Varisankya account (the Google Account you used to sign in).
3.  We will verify your request and permanently delete your account and all associated data (including subscription records and preferences) within 30 days.

**Data that will be deleted:**
* User ID and authentication tokens.
* Email address stored in our database.
* All user-generated subscription data and expense records.

**Data retention:**
* No data is retained after the deletion request is processed.
